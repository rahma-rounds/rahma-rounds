What is Rahma Rounds?

Rahma Rounds is dedicated to supporting young muslim students who want to pursue medicine by providing opportunities and resources, aiming to help sanctify their resolve and fortify their path. We serve undergraduate students, medical students, residents, and doctors by incorporating the brotherhood of Islam striving to create a community as well as educate others on the Islamic path of medicine.

Mentorship Program

Our Mentorship Program bridges the gap between aspiration and achievement in medicine by pairing undergraduates with medical students who offer invaluable insights on application cycles, class selections, MCAT preparation, and more. We create connections based on shared study habits and personality types, ensuring relevant and effective mentorship for both traditional and non-traditional paths. Flexible communication options and personalized pairing preferences enhance the mentorship experience. Our structured hierarchy links undergraduates with medical students, medical students with residents, and residents with doctors, fostering supportive and tailored guidance for every step of the medical career journey.

Panels

We provide comprehensive insights into the medical profession through interviews with doctors from various specialties, open to all stages of the mentorship hierarchy. These sessions enlighten undergraduates about medical practice, covering motivations for choosing medicine, the journey from undergraduate studies, balancing work and family life, and integrating religion with profession. Attendees can engage in Q&A sessions, explore different specialties, and gain valuable insights from practicing physicians. Interested students may contact doctors via email for shadowing opportunities or further inquiries, depending on the doctor's availability.

Fiqh Talks

Our initiative bridges Islam and medicine, intertwining Islamic jurisprudence (fiqh) with modern medical practice. We discuss permissible and impermissible medical treatments in Islam, showcasing research by Muslim doctors and scientists. Infographics highlight key concepts and statistics in Muslim healthcare. We explore Islamic medicine's rich history, including prophetic medicine, and provide guidance on treating Muslim patients while respecting their religious beliefs. Addressing challenges faced by Muslim doctors, we foster dialogue and support networks.
